Getting started takes two minutes.

Ask your developer (or your AI agent) to run one command. That's the entire setup. After that, you can ask your agent to do any of the use cases above in plain English.

  • 1 Install headlessdev \u2014 your developer runs npx headlessdev init once
  • 2 Ask in plain English \u2014 tell your AI agent what you need: "Check our competitor's pricing page and summarize the changes"
  • 3 Get results \u2014 your agent browses the page, extracts the data, and gives you a structured answer
